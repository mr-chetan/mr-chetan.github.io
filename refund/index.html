Refund & Returns Policy

Our policy regarding refunds and service adjustments

Effective Date: September 26, 2025
Last Updated: September 26, 2025

1. Introduction

This Refund & Returns Policy outlines the terms and conditions for refunds and adjustments related to services provided by Mr. Chetan. We are committed to delivering high-quality services and ensuring customer satisfaction.

2. Service Categories

Our services include but are not limited to:

  • Web Development & Design
  • Mobile Application Development
  • WordPress Development
  • Website Maintenance & Support
  • Video Editing Services
  • Graphic Design
  • Consulting Services

3. Refund Eligibility

3.1 Eligible Situations

You may be eligible for a refund in the following circumstances:

  • Service not delivered within agreed timeline without valid justification
  • Delivered service significantly deviates from agreed specifications
  • Technical issues prevent service completion despite our best efforts
  • Cancellation request made before work has commenced

3.2 Non-Eligible Situations

Refunds will not be provided in the following cases:

  • Change of mind after service delivery
  • Client fails to provide necessary materials or information
  • Service delivered as per agreed specifications
  • Client requests changes beyond original scope
  • Third-party service failures beyond our control

4. Refund Process

4.1 Request Timeline

Refund requests must be submitted within 7 days of service delivery or project completion. Requests submitted after this period will not be considered.

4.2 How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please:

  1. Contact us via email at contact@mrchetan.com
  2. Include your project details and payment reference
  3. Clearly explain the reason for the refund request
  4. Provide any supporting documentation

4.3 Processing Time

We will review your refund request within 3-5 business days. If approved, the refund will be processed within 7-10 business days to your original payment method.

5. Partial Refunds

In certain situations, partial refunds may be considered:

  • When part of the service has been delivered successfully
  • For milestone-based projects where some phases are complete
  • When additional work beyond scope has been completed
  • In cases where revisions exceed the agreed limit

6. Revision Policy

Before requesting a refund, please consider our revision policy:

  • Most projects include 2-3 rounds of revisions
  • Minor adjustments are typically accommodated at no extra cost
  • Major changes may require additional payment
  • We strive to meet your requirements through revisions first

7. Project Cancellation

7.1 Client-Initiated Cancellation

  • Full refund if no work has commenced
  • Partial refund based on work completed if project is in progress
  • No refund if project is 80% or more complete

7.2 Service Provider Cancellation

If we need to cancel a project due to unforeseen circumstances, you will receive a full refund for any payments made.

8. Dispute Resolution

If you're not satisfied with our refund decision:

  1. Contact us to discuss the matter further
  2. We'll review the case with fresh perspective
  3. If needed, we may involve a neutral third party for mediation
  4. Our goal is always to reach a fair resolution

9. Warranty and Support

We provide the following post-delivery support:

  • 30-day bug fix warranty for web development projects
  • Technical support for initial setup and deployment
  • Documentation and training materials
  • Ongoing maintenance services (separate agreement)

10. Force Majeure

We are not liable for delays or inability to perform services due to circumstances beyond our control, including but not limited to natural disasters, government actions, or technical failures of third-party services.

11. Policy Updates

We reserve the right to update this Refund & Returns Policy at any time. Changes will be effective immediately upon posting on our website.
